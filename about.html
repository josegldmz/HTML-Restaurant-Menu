Hello! My name is Jose Galdamez. Our first family-owned Thai restaurant was opened back in 1964 and we have been happily serving (plates) to our communities ever since. We our glad to have the privelegde to serve our homemade dishes and share our family recipe with each and every one of you. It is our honor to uphold and provide great food and service to our guests as this restaurant would not be where it is without the support of our customers.

Our first restaurant that opened was located in Los Angeles, CA. Our family, being a generation of cooks, decided to bring our families unique recipe to the city of Los Angeles. The diversity of cultural foods in this city allowed the restaurant to thrive and gain popularity amongst its community.
